## 1. ASU Merit Scholarships (Automatic)

### New American University Scholarship
- **Amount:** Varies based on GPA, competencies, campus, and residency; up to **$8,000/semester** ($16,000/year) for the highest tier (President's Award)
- **Deadline:** Automatic upon admission ‚Äî reviewed through **May 1, 2026** for Fall 2026
- **Requirements:**
  - First-time, first-year student entering ASU directly after high school
  - Full-time, on-campus enrollment
  - Based on high school GPA in core competencies + number of competencies completed
  - ACT/SAT scores NOT required but may increase award (scores must arrive by May 1)
- **Renewal:** 30 credit hours/year + 3.0 cumulative GPA
- **How to Apply:** No separate application ‚Äî automatic upon admission
- **Estimator Tool:** https://financialaid.asu.edu/estimator
- **Link:** https://tuition.asu.edu/new-american-university

### ASU Commitment Scholarship (Out-of-State Students Only)
- **Amount:** Varies (stackable with New American University Scholarship)
- **Deadline:** Automatic upon admission
- **Requirements:**
  - First-time, first-year out-of-state U.S. citizen
  - Family income ‚â§ $200,000
  - Full-time undergraduate, on-campus
- **Link:** https://financialaid.asu.edu/estimator

### ASU ScholarshipUniverse
- **What:** ASU's centralized scholarship portal matching students with hundreds of university, college, and external scholarships
- **Access:** Log in with ASURITE ID at https://asu.scholarshipuniverse.com/
- **Action:** Create a profile and complete it thoroughly to be matched with relevant scholarships
- **Note:** Many W. P. Carey and departmental scholarships are accessed through this portal

## 3. Accounting-Specific Scholarships (W. P. Carey)

The School of Accountancy at W. P. Carey offers departmental scholarships funded by private businesses, corporations, foundations, alumni, and individual donors. Completing the W. P. Carey General Scholarship Application automatically puts you in consideration for these.

### Available Accounting Scholarships (via General Application):
Specific named scholarships are selected by the School of Accountancy after the general application deadline passes. Candidates are matched based on GPA, leadership, community service, financial need, and residency.

### Deloitte Foundation Accounting Scholars Program (ASU is a Participating University!)
- **Amount:** **100% tuition coverage** for one academic year (master's level ‚Äî plan ahead for future)
- **Requirements:**
  - Pursuing undergraduate accounting degree (finance/business with accounting minor also eligible)

- **Note:** This is primarily for the master's program transition. ASU's W. P. Carey School of Business is a **participating university**. Worth planning for after undergrad.
- **Link:** https://www.deloitte.com/us/en/about/deloitte-foundation/deloitte-foundation-accounting-cpa-scholars-program.html

- **Amount:**
  - **Public University Students:** $2,000 (for seniors & master's students at ASU, U of A, NAU)
  - **High School Students:** $1,000 (for students pursuing accounting in Arizona)
- **Deadline:** Applications available in **late October** each year; contact ASU's School of Accountancy for specific deadlines
- **Requirements:**
  - Arizona resident
  - Majoring in accounting (for university scholarships)
  - Transcript, essay, and recommendations required
- **How to Apply:** https://www.ascpa.com/scholarships
- **Note:** If Lena is still a high school senior, the $1,000 high school scholarship may be applicable NOW
